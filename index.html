Our Story

A Boutique Approach
to Breeding Excellence

Poe Racing Stable LLC was founded on the belief that the most enduring success in thoroughbred racing comes not from volume, but from vision. We are a boutique breeding and ownership operation — selective in our mares, deliberate in our matings, and unwavering in our commitment to producing horses with the physical tools and mental fortitude to compete at the highest levels.

Our methodology centers on deep pedigree analysis, conformation evaluation, and an intimate understanding of each individual horse. We don't chase trends. We identify talent early, develop it patiently, and let the results speak for themselves on the track.

What sets Poe Racing apart is our hands-on approach. Every decision — from selecting the right stallion for a mare to choosing the right trainer for a young horse — is made with care, experience, and genuine passion for the sport.
