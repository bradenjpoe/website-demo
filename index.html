Our Story

A Boutique Approach
to Breeding Excellence

Poe Racing Stable LLC was founded from a place of true passion for racing and love for the animals that make it possible. We operate with a belief that the most enduring success in thoroughbred racing comes not from volume, but from vision and from treating our barn as a family.

We are a boutique breeding and ownership operation — selective in our mares, deliberate in our matings, and unwavering in our commitment to producing horses with the physical tools and mental fortitude to compete at the highest levels. While success is our ultimate goal, nothing exceeds our commitment to providing the healthiest and safest environments for our horses to thrive both during and after their careers are over.

Our methodology centers on deep pedigree analysis, conformation evaluation, and an intimate understanding of each individual horse. We don't chase trends. We identify talent early, develop it patiently, and let the results speak for themselves on the track.

What sets Poe Racing apart is our hands-on approach. Every decision — from selecting the right stallion for a mare to choosing the right trainer for a young horse — is made with care, experience, and genuine passion for the sport.

Hedonism (KY) Curlin - Forbidden Kiss, by Touch Gold · 2017 · Bay

Originally a $40,000 purchase, Hedonism is a mare whose exceptional quality was recognized by the industry at the highest level. She was sold to Taylor Made Farm in 2026 after producing multiple black type performers, a testament to our stable's ability to build mares through precise breeding and attention to detail.

Crafty Tigress (OH) Hold That Tiger - Crafty Crypto, by Crafty Prospector · 2007 · Chestnut

Crafty Tigress was always a 9-time winner in 54 races pursing $109,000. As a miler she stamped her offspring with endurance and stamina, evolving into a tremendously productive broodmare. Her progeny (all active) have collectively exceeded $750,000 in career earnings and are black type in sprints and two-turn routes demonstrating her versatility as a producer.
